About me

Hi there! I’m a defence and diplomacy reporter with the South China Morning Post’s China Desk. I have been with the Hong Kong paper since 2020.

I cover how Beijing manages its military diplomacy, contested claims in the South China Sea, Taiwan policy, national security and influence abroad. I have also covered the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics and China’s COVID-19 restrictions.

At the Post, I have also been rotated to various news desks, including Culture, Sports, Video and City. For them, I reported on protests in Hong Kong, the city’s subculture and the Tokyo Olympics. I have also written human-interest stories for the Culture Desk, some of which you can find in this portfolio.

I have also written for the Korea Times in South Korea during my three-month exchange to Seoul from 2022 to 2023 and for the foreign news desk of Singapore’s Mothership.

I speak Mandarin, English and Cantonese, and can read and write Chinese and English.
